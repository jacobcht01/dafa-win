DafaBet is licensed by the **Isle of Man Gambling Supervision Commission**, one of the most respected regulatory bodies in the online gambling industry. This licence means DafaBet is subject to strict rules around fair play, player fund protection, and responsible gambling.

Indian gambling law is complex. There is no central law prohibiting individuals from placing bets with offshore-licensed operators. The Public Gambling Act of 1867 regulates physical gambling establishments — it does not address online betting with foreign-licensed sites. Individual states may have their own regulations.
