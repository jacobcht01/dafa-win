## DafaBet India Registration: Step-by-Step Guide

### Step 1: Visit DafaBet India
Open your browser and go to the official DafaBet India website. Look for the **"Join Now"** or **"Register"** button in the top-right corner of the homepage.

### Step 2: Fill in Your Personal Details
The registration form asks for:
- **Full name** (as it appears on your government ID)
- **Date of birth** (you must be **18 or older** to register)
- **Email address** (must be valid — you'll need to verify it)
- **Mobile number** (Indian number accepted — +91)
- **Password** (minimum 8 characters, include numbers and letters)

### Step 3: Choose Your Country and Currency
- **Country:** India
- **Currency:** INR (Indian Rupee)

Select your state from the dropdown. This is used for compliance purposes.

### Step 4: Enter a Bonus Code (Optional)
In the **"Promo Code"** field, enter **DAWIN200** to activate the 200% welcome bonus up to ₹20,000. If no field appears, the bonus may be applied automatically.

### Step 5: Accept Terms and Submit
Check the boxes confirming you:
- Are 18 years or older
- Accept DafaBet's Terms and Conditions
- Accept the Privacy Policy

Click **"Register"** or **"Create Account"** to submit.

### Step 6: Verify Your Email
DafaBet sends a verification email to the address you provided. Click the link in that email to activate your account. Check your spam folder if it doesn't arrive within 5 minutes.

## DafaBet Verification: KYC Documents Required

DafaBet follows **KYC (Know Your Customer)** regulations and will request identity documents before processing your first withdrawal. Accepted documents:

| Document Type | Accepted Forms |
|--------------|----------------|
| Identity Proof | Aadhaar card, PAN card, Passport, Voter ID, Driving Licence |
| Address Proof | Aadhaar card, utility bill (less than 3 months old), bank statement |

**When is KYC required?** Typically triggered when you request your first withdrawal or if your account activity flags a review. It's best to complete KYC proactively to avoid delays.

**Upload method:** Documents can be uploaded directly in your account settings under "Verification" or sent to DafaBet's support team.
