## Cricket Betting Markets Explained

### Match Winner
The most popular market — pick which team wins. Simple, widely available, best liquidity.

### Top Batsman
Predict which player scores the highest in the match or innings. Favourites are usually the team's #3 or #4 batter.

### Top Bowler
Which bowler takes the most wickets? In T20 cricket, death-over specialists often win this — but form matters.

### Over/Under Totals
Will the total runs in the match be over or under a set line? Common in T20 where 300–350 run totals are standard in IPL.

### First Wicket Method
How falls the first wicket: caught, bowled, LBW, run out, stumped, or hit wicket? A specialist market for cricket experts.

### Toss Winner
Predict the coin toss result. A 50/50 market — useful in combinations or for fun, not a core strategy market.

### Cricket Satta Markets
"Cricket satta" (सट्टा / సట్టా) is the informal term for cricket betting in India. While we use "betting" throughout this guide, satta refers to the same activity — placing a wager on cricket match outcomes. Always use licensed operators like DafaBet rather than unlicensed local bookies.

## Cricket Betting Tips for Indian Players

1. **Research pitch and weather:** Dew in evening matches heavily favours chasing teams in T20 cricket. Check match-day conditions.
2. **Form over reputation:** Even the best batters have lean patches. Check recent form, not career averages.
3. **Toss matters:** In day-night matches, teams winning the toss frequently choose to field — factor this into live markets when toss result is announced.
4. **Value over favourites:** Blindly backing favourites loses money long-term. Look for value — teams priced at 2.0 or better that you believe have a real 55%+ chance of winning.
5. **Line shopping:** Check DafaBet odds against one competitor. Even small differences matter over hundreds of bets.
6. **Bankroll management:** Never bet more than 5% of your total betting budget on a single match.
