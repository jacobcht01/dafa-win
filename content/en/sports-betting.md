## Sports Betting Markets Explained

**Match Winner (1X2 / Moneyline):** Pick the winning team or player. The most straightforward bet.

**Handicap Betting:** One team receives a fictional "head start" in runs, goals, or points. Useful when a strong favourite makes match winner odds unattractive.

**Over/Under (Totals):** Bet on whether a statistical measure (total runs, total goals) will be above or below a set line.

**Accumulator (Multi/Parlay):** Combine 3+ selections into one bet. Higher risk — all selections must win — but significantly higher reward.

**Both Teams to Score (Football):** Will both teams score at least once? Common football market not available in cricket.
